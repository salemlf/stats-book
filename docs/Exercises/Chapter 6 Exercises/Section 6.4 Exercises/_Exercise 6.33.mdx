The proportion of impurities in certain ore samples is a random variable $Y$ with a density function given by

$$
f(y) =
\begin{cases}
    (3/2)y^2 + y, & 0 \leq y \leq 1,\\
    0, & \text{elsewhere.}
\end{cases}
$$

The dollar value of such samples is $U = 5 - (Y / 2)$.
Find the probability density function for $U$.
